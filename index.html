About

Who is
JC

JC didn't wait for permission.

It started in Manipal — late nights, packed rooms, and a nickname that stuck. His friends called him JC, short for Johaan Chanda, and the name followed him everywhere it mattered.

After university, he returned home to Chennai for a year and a half — not to slow down, but to build his foundation. To sharpen the craft. To develop the production skills and performance instincts that would carry him further than any stage he'd stood on before.

But Chennai was just the beginning.

Now based in Bangalore, JC is operating on a different level. Shaped by the emotional depth of Dosem, the raw energy of Eric Prydz, the hypnotic pull of Adriatique, the melodic soul of Marsh and Tinlicker — his progressive and melodic house sound hits hard and feels even harder. This is music built not just for India, but for the world.

The city changed. The hunger didn't.

Live

The
Experience

JC doesn't just play music — he builds worlds. From intimate house sessions in Bangalore to open-format club sets, the experience is always the same: euphoric, considered, and impossible to forget.

Series

JC Sessions

An intimate recorded series hosted in Bangalore — 10 to 15 people, one hour, no compromise. The format distils everything JC does into its purest form. Raw, personal, and deeply musical.

Club Sets

Live Performance

Extended DJ sets built for dancefloors. JC reads the room and builds the arc — from atmospheric openers to peak-time euphoria. Direct support for Anyasa. Every set is different. Every set is complete.
